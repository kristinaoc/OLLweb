Portraits

Portraits at Of Light and Lens Photography

I love creating and capturing memories. It's a special kind of magic that allows the light in an instant to be captured forever. Catching the feelings and emotion of a time and a place.

I'd love to capture some magic for you.

Families, couples, individuals, pets. Casual or formal. In the studio or on location. Or even combine both.

Can't come to the studio but want studio-style portraits? I can bring my pop-up studio to you!

Packages start at $600 including shoot time, editing, private online gallery for image selection, and a basic print package.

All shoots include:

  • Pre shoot consultation meeting (in person or by phone or web video conference).
  • 1-2 hours shoot time in Brisbane area.
  • Choice of studio or on location (pop up studio can bring the studio to you!).
  • Private web gallery for image selection.
