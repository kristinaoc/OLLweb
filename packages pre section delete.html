Info & Pricing

The price bit

I hate it when services aren't upfront about their pricing, don't you? Or force you to enquire and wait around for them to get back to you. So here's some information about mine. Yes, you'll see I'm not the cheapest - my prices match my quality service and products.

Packages

The base component of any package is the photo session fee of $350. This is included in all my packages, and this portion is payable prior to the session. It does not include any printed or digital photos. The balance of the package fee is payable on finalisation of your image order.

You can upgrade/downgrade your selected packages when you see your order, or even customise your package to suit. It does help to have some idea of what you are aiming for prior to the photo session, so I can make sure I take suitable images. Think a single large print for your living room wall compared to an album of images. But you don't have to decide till after the session when you see you images.

How to book

Email me to enquire or book. A non-refundable $250 session fee is due 14 days prior to the scheduled photo session. This is deductable from the total package cost. Balance of total package payment is due 14 days after the scheduled photo session, or upon placement of a print or print package order, whichever is sooner. I have a mobile card reader to facilitate paying the balance in person by credit card if this is more convenient than online payment. If a session must be rescheduled due to inclement weather or illness, the deposit is transferable provided maximum practicable notice is given. View detailed Terms and Conditions here.
