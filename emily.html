Studio: Emily

Emily in the Studio

Emily is a Middle Eastern Dancer specialising in Egyptian styles of bellydance, including cabaret and folkloric styles.

As well as teaching and performing north of Brisbane, she frequently travels to Egypt to hone her craft. In November 2018 she placed first and second in The Nile Group Festival performance competitions.
