What we do at Of Light and Lens Photography

At Of Light and Lens Photography I offer a range of photographic services, from studio portraits to informal lifestyle photography sessions. I also specialise in dance photography, both in the studio and onstage. For the business person, I offer product and personal branding and social media content building shoots. Let me weave my magic with light to make you stand out from the crowd.

Photo sessions are on location or at my home studio. Whatever works best for you. I can even bring my pop-up studio to you! This is ideal if you'd love the studio look but can't travel to me, or want an extended shoot with a mix of studio and lifestyle images. I'm based in Brisbane, and am happy to travel to the Gold and Sunshine Coasts and Ipswich.
