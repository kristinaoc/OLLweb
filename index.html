Images that make an impact

Hi! I'm Kristina, photographer at Of Light and Lens Photography. I'm Brisbane based and specialise in capturing images that make an impact.

Let's start planning

More than just a photo

Time for some extra special images? Something to commemorate this age, this stage, or this occasion? I can customise a photo session to create just what you're after, and help you choose prints, wall art or albums ready for you to display and share.

Fun sessions

Getting great photos shouldn't be a chore. Let's all have some fun in front of the camera. Don't worry about the kids mucking around - I know how to harness that energy for fabulous photos.

All family members catered for, even the feathered, furred or scaly!

Your pets are part of the family as well, so yes, of course we can incorporate them into your family session. Or put them in the spotlight and book a session just for them.

Dance - studio or stage

I have a special interest in dance photography, and am available for performance and studio sessions.

Do you do headshots?

Yes! From simple, clean studio shots to natural "oh, you've just caught me going about my day" kinds of photos, I can help.

Superb products for your precious images

There are a range of high quality products available for purchase. Wall art options include traditional matted and framed prints, canvases, and modern acrylic mounted and metal prints. The vibrancy of the prints is stunning, and product workmanship and longevity of the highest standard.
