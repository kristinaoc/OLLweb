Book your studio session

I'm a dancer myself, so I understand the visual art you are creating. I have a natural flair for capturing the moment, letting the energy and emotion burst through.

Studio sessions start at $195 and include up to 3h studio time, allowing for several costume changes. We preview your images as we go, so you can check your dance technique and not be disappointed later by not quite nailing the technical stuff.

On location dance portrait sessions

If the studio is not the look you're after, I offer on location dance portrait sessions as well. Pricing depends on the concept, call or email me to discuss.

Performance photography

Have a concert coming up? Let me document it! Live performance photography captures the magic memories, and having a professional do the job ensures quality images in challenging lighting conditions.
