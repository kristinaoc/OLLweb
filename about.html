About

Hi! I'm Kristina! Photographer at Of Light and Lens Photography.

I've long loved photography, and recently I have taken it from hobby to career. My background is in Science, Chemistry to be specific, and I worked in industry for nearly 20 years before grabbing the opportunity to follow my passion. But this career change has not meant leaving all I have learned behind - Photography is in fact very scientific, and I can marry the technical and the creative.

I love to capture all sorts of images. From candid to studio portraits, from clean product shots to shots that showcase your product in use. I love to explore all that can be captured with light and lens.
