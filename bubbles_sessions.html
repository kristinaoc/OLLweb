The Bubbles Experience

Kids love bubbles

Kids love bubbles. Think of all the delighted smiles, the happy laughter. Perfect ingredients for some fabulous images that will look fantastic on display in your home.

What is The Bubble Experience?

These are mini in-studio sessions, with lots of bubbles. Short and snappy at 20-30 minutes, not long enough for the kids to get bored. But long enough for some great images.

The images from these sessions are unposed, spontaneous, full of movement, smiles and wonder. I just snap away while the kids have fun dancing in the bubbles.

Choosing your images

Image selection is done at the end of the session, so you don't have to worry about making time to come back on another day. Your chosen images will then be retouched ready for printing and sharing the digital images.

I know you will be eager to get your images, and so I endeavour to have them to you within 14 days of the session. (Note: upgrading to wall art or albums may result in an additional week or two wait on these items)
