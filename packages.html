Info & Pricing

Packages

The base component of any package is the photo session fee of $350. This is included in all my packages, and this portion is payable prior to the session. It covers my time in preparation for the session, the session itself and initial processing and backup of your images. It does not include any printed or digital photos. The balance of the package fee is payable on finalisation of your image order.

You can upgrade/downgrade your selected packages when you see your order, or even customise your package to suit. It does help to have some idea of what you are aiming for prior to the photo session, so I can make sure I take suitable images. Think a single large print for your living room wall compared to an album of images. But you don't have to decide till after the session when you see you images.
