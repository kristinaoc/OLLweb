Info & Pricing

The three main phases of any photo session are the consultation, the photo shoot itself, and image selectin and delivery.

Pre shoot consultation

Once you have booked and paid your deposit, a pre shoot consultation is arranged at a mutually convenient time. This can be in person or over the phone, depending on your preference. We can go over what your aims for the photo shoot, and discuss clothes, makeup, props, locations and shoot times to plan a photo shoot that will achieve the images you desire.

After the consultation, I'll email you a summary of everything we discussed so you don't have to worry about forgetting anything!

The Photo Shoot

Photo day! It will be a heap of fun, don't worry. I'll put you at ease. We can listen to music, or not. Dance, or not. (Actually, if it's a dance shoot you've booked, we will dance!) We will experiment and get a bit silly, but, never fear - anything too silly will never see the light of day. Unless you want it to!

After we get the sillies out of our systems, everyone will relax and I can help you look your best for the camera.

Image delivery

Images are presented within 14 days of your shoot in my password protected Pixieset hosted galleries. Here you can choose your package-included prints and digital downloads, and even add extras if you choose.

Delivery times for print products depends on the item. There are longer lead times for some large display products and albums compared to standard lustre prints for example, however I aim to despatch your order within 14-21 days of receiving final confirmed order and payment.

How to book

Email me to enquire or book. You can pre-pay with Paypal using the links in the pricing information below, or I can invoice you. A non-refundable $250 booking fee is due 14 days prior to the scheduled photo session. This is deductable from the total package cost. Balance of total package payment is due 14 days after the scheduled photo session, or upon placement of a print or print package order, whichever is sooner. I have a mobile card reader to facilitate paying the balance in person by credit card if this is more convenient than online payment. If a session must be rescheduled due to inclement weather or illness, the deposit is transferable provided maximum practicable notice is given. View detailed Terms and Conditions here.
